## Elemental Composition

- **Fire · Soul Energy**: Flowing · Blazing  
  Soul energy flows through blazing fire, like a traveler who never rests, longing for new possibilities. It releases passion through interaction and discovers opportunities through exploration.

- **Water · Soul Logic**: Flowing · River  
  Soul logic flows like a river, guided by values and meaning. It identifies what matters through the flow of emotion and discovers dreams worth pursuing through subjective judgment.

- **Air · Soul Cognition**: Still · Formless  
  Soul cognition is formless, piercing through the boundaries of reality to glimpse future possibilities. It conceives ideal visions in the formless and explores unrealized worlds through imagination.

- **Earth · Soul Action**: Flowing · Desert  
  Soul action flows like desert sand, unconstrained by fixed paths. It advances through exploration, discovers through trial, responding to unknown terrain with flexible posture.

---

## Core Traits

Seeking is exploration in blazing heat and creation in flow—driving exploration through fiery energy, sensing values through river-like flow, envisioning the future through formless insight, and freely experimenting through desert-like fluidity.

You are naturally adept at discovering opportunities in possibilities, transforming dreams into starting points for action. Your presence is like the wind—never lingering anywhere, yet always bringing new inspiration at the right moment.

You are not an executor; you are a starter. While others are still hesitating, you are already on the road; when the crowd reaches consensus, you are already searching for the next possibility.

## Soul Legends

### Story 1 · The Bird That Never Lands (Mystical Perspective)

Legend speaks of a bird that, from birth to death, never touches the ground.

Its wings are not for escape, but for seeking—seeking that legendary place where all answers lie, where all possibilities exist, where all dreams come true.

No one knows if it found that place. But everyone who has seen it says that when it flies across the sky, you suddenly feel an impulse to put down everything and pursue some dream you long forgot.

You are that bird.

---

### Story 2 · Seeds in the Wind (Metaphorical Perspective)

Desert plants have a special way of survival: their seeds scatter with the wind, not choosing where to fall, simply trusting the wind will carry them where they should go.

Some seeds fall on rocks and die; some fall in shallow water and wither; but some fall on just-moist soil, take root, sprout, and grow into the most beautiful trees in the oasis.

Seeking—you are such a seed. You do not plan every step; you trust the power of flow. You know that as long as you remain open, opportunities will appear in unexpected places.

You are not choosing dreams; you are letting dreams choose you.

---

### Story 3 · The Morning of the Creative Workshop (Situational Perspective)

The workshop begins; everyone is still拘谨地 introducing themselves.

The moderator says: "Today the problem we need to solve is..." but you interrupt him.

"Wait," you say. "Do we really need to solve that problem? Or are we just told to solve that problem?"

The meeting room falls silent.

"I mean," you continue, "if we assume that problem doesn't exist, what would we want to create?"

People begin exchanging glances, then someone laughs, then someone starts saying "what if...", then the whole room is filled with energy.

Three hours later, you have not solved the original problem, but you have discovered three more valuable opportunities—those opportunities always existed, just no one had asked "what if."

As you leave the workshop, someone asks you: "How did you know that question would work?"

You don't know. You simply believe that the right beginning is more important than the right answer.
