## Technical Details (For the Curious)

Timeflow is based on **Information Density Theory**:

```
λ = ρ / ρ̄

λ = Time transformation coefficient (your current "time flow rate")
ρ = Current information density (effective interactions per unit time)
ρ̄ = Personal historical average density
```

When λ > 10, 1 physical hour ≈ 10 business hours. Not mysticism. Just your brain running at peak efficiency.
